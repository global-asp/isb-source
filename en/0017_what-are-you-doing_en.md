# What Are You Doing?

Little bear, what are you doing?

I am climbing.

##

I am running.

##
Little duck, what are you doing?

I am swimming.

##
Little frog, what are you doing?

I am sleeping.

##
Little beaver, what are you doing?

I am reading.

##
Big animal, what are you doing?

I am eating.

##

Nothing. I am a chair.
